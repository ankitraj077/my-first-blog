Simple Definition of sentiment

: an attitude or opinion
: feelings of love, sympathy, kindness, etc.

Explanation: Examples of sentiment in a sentence
His criticism of the court's decision expresses a sentiment that is shared by many people. (an expression of anti-war sentiments)
She likes warmth and sentiment in a movie.
You have to be tough to succeed in the business world. There's no room for sentiment.
